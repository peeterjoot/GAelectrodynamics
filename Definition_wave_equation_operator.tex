\makedefinition{d'Alembertian (wave equation) operator.}{dfn:continuity:120}{
In this book, the symbol \( \dAlembertian \) is used to represent the \textit{d'Alembertian (wave equation) operator}, with a positive sign on the Laplacian term
\begin{equation*}
\dAlembertian =
\conjstgrad
\stgrad
=
\spacegrad^2 - \inv{c^2} \PDSq{t}{}.
\end{equation*}
} % definition
